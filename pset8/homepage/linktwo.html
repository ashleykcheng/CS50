What Are Rubik's Cubes?

What is a Rubik’s Cube? The original Rubik’s Cube was a puzzle that has 6 faces covered with 9 stickers. There are 6 different color stickers: white, yellow, red, orange, blue, and green. The Rubik’s Cube is solved when each face has only one color. A fun fact is a Rubik’s Cube has 43 quintillion possible combinations (43,000,000,000,000,000,000).
